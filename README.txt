RoCAPTCHA plugin made by Vojtěch Oram alias Flaiming

Instructions:
1. unpack zip
2. place rocaptchalib.php in your main forum dir
3. place inc/plugins/rocaptcha.php in your plugins folder
4. place content of inc/languages/ to your languages folder
4. activate the plugin
5. enter your keys in the settings and turn on (you should turn off other active captchas in General configuration.)
6. you are done

Note: You do need to sign up for an account at http://rocaptcha.com to get your keys (private/public).
